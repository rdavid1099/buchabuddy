BuchaBuddy started as an easy-to-use inventory management tool to help kombucha homebrewers of all experience levels and backgrounds enjoy the art of making their very own batches of kombucha. We are focused on taking all of the guesswork out of brewing so you can let your creative juices flow and brew up healthy, tasty kombucha.

BuchaBuddy is an open-source web application created and maintained by kombucha lovers around the world. We are always looking for help improving BuchaBuddy and spreading the joy of homebrewing to people everywhere. If you are experienced or just starting to code, we would love to have you help us grow. Check out our GitHub repository and be sure to follow us on Twitter to stay on top upcoming features.

We want to sincerely thank every user, developer and kombucha drinker who benefits from this tool. Without your support and dedication, none of this would be possible. Thank you.
